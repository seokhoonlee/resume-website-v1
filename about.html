About me Get to know more about me!

1993 Sep 03: Born to be wild
I was born in Berlin, Germany where my parents were studying their masters. But I was only there for 3 months, so I can't speak German at all. Still, I had some sort of "global exposure" from the very beginning. By the way, I am the one on the right!


2007 Mar 15: Singapore, Away and Alone
At the age of 14, my parents "sent" me to study in Singapore alone. That was pretty harsh at that moment but, on hindsight, I think it helped me much in terms of being independent and disciplined. Currently, I am bilingual (English and Korean). So, I guess Singapore education worked out just fine for me. The photo was taken while I was preparing to come to Singapore and basically I won some award for math (one of the many to come)!


2012 Aug 01: NUS Electrical Engineering
Since young, I have always been more of a science person so I decided to pursue the degree of Engineering. I was kinda into handphones and programming at the same time. Therefore, I chose Electrical Engineering. Do I ever regret? Nope! I am having much fun studying Electrical Engineering.


2014 May 15: Silicon Valley, Here I go!
NUS Overseas College is a one-year program that allows students to study and intern at start-up company at the same time. The company that I have been accepted to is a Saas company whose main product is API. I hope it will be a fruitful experience and I definitely can't wait for it!
